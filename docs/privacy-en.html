Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

Introduction

Tabiin Academy respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app.

Information Collection

We collect only the minimum necessary information for app functionality:

  • Learning statistics (number of questions answered, correct and incorrect answers)
  • App settings (language, sound, notifications)
  • Achievement and progress data

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number. If you use Sign in with Apple, the app receives only an anonymous identifier to sync data across your devices.

When you add a profile photo, the app accesses your photo library only with your permission; we do not upload these images to our servers — they are stored on your device and in your iCloud if sync is enabled.

Data Usage

Collected information is used for:

  • Displaying your learning progress
  • Personalizing the learning experience
  • Improving app functionality
  • Sending notifications (only if you have enabled them)

Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device. When signed in with Sign in with Apple, your learning statistics sync via iCloud (Apple CloudKit) for access across devices. We do not share your data with third-party companies and do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Third Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • App Store — for app updates and rating
  • iOS system notifications — for study reminders (at your choice)
  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit) — for syncing progress when using Sign in with Apple

These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View your data in the app’s statistics section
  • Reset all data through settings
  • Disable notifications at any time
  • Delete the app — all local data will be removed

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any significant changes through app updates or on this page.
